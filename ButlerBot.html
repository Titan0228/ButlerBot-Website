Descriptions: Ultron 2000 is your very own dream personal butler. He can perform any manual tasks that you will ever be required to do on your own. If you are tired of constantly having to clean an extremely messy house, Ultron 2000 can solve your problems. His AI is programmed to clean up any messes, organize messy rooms, and even act as your best friend in lonely times. He can also navigate up and down stairs with his new and improved treads. Not only can Ultron thoroughly clean your house, but he can also cook your meals for you. If you are tired of constantly eating bland food, then your personal butler Ultron 2000 can cook five-star quality foods without breaking your bank. He has 3000+ unique recipes programmed into him so you can eat different foods every day. He is also capable performing the most menial and boring chores such as taking out the trash, cleaning your dishes, clearing the table, and cleaning out the kitchen.
Amazing Features:
  • Modular arms: You can attach and reattach some parts when necessary.
  • Vacuum arm: Helps you clean up all around your house.
  • Control Panel: Your robot will never malfunction and cause mayhem, one touch control that makes everyone able to control it.
  • Face display: Tells you the robot’s emotion.
  • Treads: Go over difficult terrain, go upstairs and downstairs.
